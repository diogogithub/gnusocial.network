What is GNU social?

GNU social, previously known as StatusNet, is a social communication software for public and private communications. It is widely supported and has a large userbase including the Free Software Foundation.

GNU social is part of the Fediverse (a portmanteau of "federation" and "universe") TODO

Where can I join GNU social?

There are many GNU social server instances online right now! That's another advantage of the federation! Some are for people in different parts of the world, some are for people with different interests, some are open to everyone.

You can search the web for GNU social instances in your country or that are run by people with similar interests to you!

I found a bug, how can I report it?

Please report any bugs using our issue tracker.

If you use IRC you can contact us on the GNU social IRC channel at #social.

Who makes GNU social?

GNU social was founded by Matt Lee, Evan Prodromou and Mikael Nordfaelth who is now the lead maintainer of the project.

How can I help?

Contribute to the project by sending a pull request to our repository :)
